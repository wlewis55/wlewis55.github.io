Face Tracking Random Image Generators

These Snap Lenses area series of Lenses that display a random image for on the user's forehead. At the time of creation, this concept was a popular trend that many people would try out and share with their friends. After seeing a few of these sent to me by my friends, I thought: "Why havent I made one of those yet?" I wound up making four lenses, each with a different theme:
