Undergraduate Capstone Senior Design Project: UofSC Tour AR

Going into my senior year of college, I knew I wanted to learn more about XR. I had been trying out VR and AR development in Unity during the summer of 2019, and I had a few ideas for a senior design project involving XR. After pitching them to my team, we decided to create an Augmented Reality Tour of our campus! The goal was to make an interface similar to Pokemon Go, where the user can use a 3D map to navigate and interact with Points of Interest. We also knew that we wanted to create an AR expereince for the user when they interact with a Point of Interest.

After completing a proof of concept and documentation for our project in the fall of 2019, the majority of our application's development took place in the spring of 2020. I designed the 3D Map, entire AR experience, the project's website, as well as the graphics and animations application's user interface. We finished this project in April/May 2020!
